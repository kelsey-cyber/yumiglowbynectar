Step-by-Step Guide

Your Daily
Ritual.

01
Prepare Your Skin
Cleanse your face thoroughly. Apply a thin layer of Elite Vanilla Milk Toner or a light, water-based serum to enable optimal EMS conductivity. Avoid oil-based products.
Prep · 2 min
02
Select Your Mode
Power on by pressing the upper button. Cycle through Mode 1 (Blue LED), Mode 2 (Red + Near-Infrared), or Mode 3 (Purple + EMS) based on your skin goal for the session.
Blue · Red · Purple + EMS
03
Begin with the Cheeks
Place the treatment head flat against your cheek. Glide slowly upward in long strokes toward the temple. Spend 2–3 minutes per cheek using gentle, continuous motion.
Cheeks · 2–3 min each
04
Move to the Forehead
Starting at the center of your forehead, glide outward toward the temples. Work from brow to hairline in smooth, upward sweeps. Focus on fine lines and expression areas.
Forehead · 2–3 min
05
Define the Jawline
Place the device under the chin and glide upward and outward along the jawline to the earlobe. This area responds particularly well to Mode 3 EMS stimulation for contouring.
Jawline · 2–3 min
06
Treat the Neck
Use upward strokes from the collarbone toward the jawline. Avoid the center of the neck. Finish with cooling mode to calm the skin and seal in the treatment.
Neck · 2 min · Avoid center
