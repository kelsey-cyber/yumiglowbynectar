How soon will I see results? +

Most users notice an immediate improvement in skin luminosity and texture after the first session — largely due to thermal and LED stimulation increasing circulation. More significant visible changes in firmness, tone, and contour are typically reported after 4–6 weeks of consistent use (3–5 times per week).

Is YumiGlow safe for sensitive skin? +

Yes. YumiGlow is designed for all skin types, including sensitive skin. We recommend beginning with Mode 2 (Blue LED + Cooling) and Level 1 intensity to allow your skin to acclimate. The cooling function is especially beneficial for reactive or redness-prone skin. If you have an active skin condition, please consult your dermatologist before use.

Can I use it with my current skincare products? +

Absolutely — in fact, YumiGlow is designed to enhance your existing serums and moisturizers. The thermal mode opens the skin's surface, allowing products to penetrate more deeply. We recommend water-based serums for use during treatment. Avoid retinols or exfoliating acids on the same application day.

What makes this different from other LED devices? +

Most at-home LED devices offer a single wavelength in a passive mask format. YumiGlow is an active, handheld precision tool combining three distinct LED wavelengths with EMS microcurrent and dual temperature control — allowing you to target specific facial zones with exactly the technology each area needs.

How long does the battery last per session? +

A full charge (2–3 hours via USB-C) provides approximately 45–60 minutes of continuous use — enough for 5–7 complete sessions. The charging base keeps the device ready between uses, making it a seamless part of your vanity setup.

What is your return policy? +

YumiGlow comes with a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with your results, contact our team for a full refund — no questions asked. Returns are complimentary; we'll send a prepaid return label. Device must be returned in its original packaging.

Can I use all three modes in one session? +

Yes — and for complete treatment, this is the recommended approach. A full session might begin with Mode 1 (Red, for warming and absorption), move to Mode 3 (Purple + EMS, for lifting work on jawline and cheeks), and finish with Mode 2 (Blue + Cooling, to soothe and seal). Total session time remains 8–10 minutes.
